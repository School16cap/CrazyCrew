about us

 

The coat of arms of the school is a shield in the form of a diamond of blue color with a golden edging, on which there are figures and the inscription "School # 16 Sarov town". The shield is crowned by a golden-colored Sarov bell tower. It is a symbol of our town, of its historical past, of spirituality. The unfolded white book in the center of the shield, symbolizing knowledge of the school subjects, is based on a globe – the image of world knowledge and interaction. On the left side of the shield is a golden branch-a symbol of life, and on the right, it is a flying butterfly of the same color representing a flight of thought, an aspiration upward. We are sure the coat of arms is full of energy, optimism and confidence in the future. So are we.

The symbol of our team We are optimists, the positive energy that we bring to people is symbolized by a cheerful smile on the background of the sun's rays. Six multi-colored dots on the edge of the cube - six team members with their own individuality. The emblem is designed in the form of a dice with bright multi-colored edges, like life itself. All this together and beyond is our team "Crazy crew".

our interests

The Museum of Nuclear Weapons It’s not a secret (well, not a very big one) that Sarov is the hometown of the Russian nuclear shield. The Museum of Nuclear Weapons is the proof. This is an amazing place where you can spend your free time in a fabulous and informative way. There you can learn the history of our city and see the nuclear weapons models of different times. There you can see the first atomic bomb of the USSR, called “Tsar bomb”

Sports Centre In Sarov, there’s a place where we often spend time. This is the sports center “IKAR”. It includes 2 large football pitches and 2 smaller ones, 2 basketball grounds and 2 volleyball fields. The center offers exercise machines outside and well-equipped indoor gyms as well. There’s also a place for skateboard fans to have a whale of time. So, you can get fit as we are and have loads of fun as we do!
